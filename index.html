Ada Lovelace

The World's First Programmer

Code with Serah was founded on October 13th, 2024 — celebrating Ada Lovelace Day, honoring the woman who wrote the world's first computer program.

Ada Lovelace wasn't just a mathematician; she was a visionary who saw the potential of computing beyond mere calculation. She imagined a future where machines could create music, art, and change the world.

Our very first event, "The T IN STEM Workshop", brought together 28 curious minds. Today, we're a thriving community of over 1,200 members.
