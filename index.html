Where It All Started

Founded on Ada Lovelace Day, October 13th, 2024. That wasn't a coincidence.

Inclusive by design

From day one, we've championed women in tech and underrepresented communities in STEAM. Inclusive education isn't a bonus. It's a right. Every person deserves access to the tools, mentorship, and opportunities that turn potential into something real.

Stay weird, stay curious

So many barriers people face don't start with access. They start with giving up on what makes them them. Code with Serah exists because the world gets better when people lean into what they love, not away from it.
