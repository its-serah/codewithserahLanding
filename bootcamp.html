The Sprint Bootcamp

Every winter and summer, we get hundreds of people in one room (well, one Zoom) and build things together. It's intense, it's messy, it's the best thing we do.

Join the Next One All Events

What actually happens

Each sprint has a different theme — we've covered everything from full-stack web apps and AI agents to machine learning, NLP, and data science. Some sprints are a few days, others stretch to two weeks. It really depends.

You join live sessions with mentors, get your hands on real tools, and start building. By the end, you walk away with something you actually made — not something you followed a tutorial for. People have submitted projects we didn't even have on the schedule, and honestly, those are usually the best ones.
