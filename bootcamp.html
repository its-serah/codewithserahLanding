The Sprint Bootcamp

Every winter and summer, we pick a theme, people sign up, and we dive in. Hands-on learning, interactive sessions, and building things together. It gets messy, but it's the best thing we do.

What actually happens

Each sprint has a different theme, from full-stack web apps and AI agents to machine learning and data science. You join live sessions, get your hands on real tools, and build something by the end. Not a tutorial project. Something you actually made.

Every sprint also comes with its own international character. You might remember Elmo, Pengu, and many more. Each one making the experience feel completely unique.
