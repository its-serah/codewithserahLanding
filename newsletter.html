Take Care of You

Little Things That Help When Everything Feels Heavy

Step away from screens

Even 10 minutes outside, a walk around the block, or just sitting by a window can make a difference. Your brain needs the break.

Create something, anything

Draw, write, doodle, code a silly project, bake something new. Creating helps process emotions in ways talking sometimes can't.

Put on your comfort playlist

Music heals. Whether it's lo-fi beats, your favorite album, or that one song that always makes you feel something, let it play.

Limit the news scroll

Staying informed matters, but doom-scrolling doesn't help anyone. Set boundaries. Check in once or twice a day, then step away.

Reach out to someone

A text to a friend, a call with family, or just being in the same room as someone you trust. You don't have to carry it alone.
